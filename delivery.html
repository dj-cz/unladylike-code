Delivery & Returns

How It Works

You can buy the products displayed here by visiting the Unladylike Code shop on RedBubble.

Redbubble is a website where independent creators can set up a shop with their designs and Redbubble takes care of the printing and delivery part.

After clicking on each product, you will be redirected to the Redbubble shop where you can make the purchase.

Delivery & Returns

Since Unladylike Code doesn't directly ship any products and the delivery is done by Redbubble, please see RedBubble delivery and returns rules.

Information about delivery for every product is also displayed on the details page of every item.

Redbubble ships products to most countries of the world.
